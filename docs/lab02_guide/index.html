Introduction

What is a KKU Plan?



KKU Plan is a website designed to help students, visitors, and newcomers explore Khon Kaen University (KKU) more easily. It serves as a friendly guide that introduces various important places across the campus in a well-organized and visually accessible way. The site typically includes information about key landmarks such as academic buildings, libraries, cafeterias, dormitories, sports facilities, parks, and other useful locations that students often look for. Each place usually comes with descriptions, photos, and sometimes directions or tips, helping users understand what each location offers and how to get there.

Library KKU

The Khon Kaen University Library, often referred to as the KKU Central Library, is one of the most important learning hubs on campus. It provides a wide range of academic resources, including physical books, digital databases, research journals, and multimedia materials that support students from every faculty. The building is designed to offer a calm, welcoming atmosphere with multiple reading zones, computer areas, group-work rooms, and quiet study floors, making it suitable for both individual and collaborative learning. Its modern facilities help students access information comfortably and efficiently.

Beyond being a place for reading, the KKU Library also serves as a center for academic development and student support. It regularly organizes workshops on research skills, reference management, academic writing, and database usage. Students can also use services like book borrowing, online catalog search, printing, scanning, and study room reservations. With its combination of resources, technology, and supportive services, the library plays a vital role in creating a rich and engaging learning environment for everyone at Khon Kaen University.




The KKU Library is also a social and cultural space where students can connect, recharge, and take breaks between classes. Many areas inside the building are designed to be comfortable and relaxing, with soft seating, air-conditioned study corners, and open spaces where students can gather casually. The library often hosts exhibitions, student activities, and academic showcases that highlight creativity and research across the university. With its blend of study-friendly facilities and community-centered activities, the KKU Library has become a place where students not only learn, but also feel a sense of belonging within the KKU campus.

Complex KKU

The KKU Complex is a large multi-purpose center located at the heart of Khon Kaen University, designed to serve as a convenient spot for students, staff, and visitors. It combines shopping, dining, and essential daily services in one place, making it one of the busiest and most practical areas on campus. Inside the complex, you can find various restaurants, cafés, convenience stores, stationery shops, and small retail outlets that provide everything from snacks and school supplies to everyday necessities. Its central location makes it a popular meeting point for students throughout the day.

Beyond basic services, the KKU Complex also supports student life through several facilities that help make campus living easier. Many people visit the complex for banking services, mobile service centers, and photocopy or printing shops, especially during busy academic periods. The area surrounding the complex is lively and active, with open spaces where students relax, socialize, or wait for class. Because it gathers so many essential services in one place, the KKU Complex plays a key role in keeping daily campus life smooth, convenient, and enjoyable for the entire KKU community.

50th Anniversary Stadium of KKU

The 50th Anniversary Stadium of Khon Kaen University is one of the university’s largest and most iconic sports facilities, built to commemorate KKU’s 50 years of establishment. The stadium features a spacious running track, a well-maintained football field, and seating areas that can accommodate large numbers of spectators during competitions and university events. Its open-air design allows for natural lighting and ventilation, making it a comfortable place for both athletes and visitors. Throughout the day, you’ll often see students jogging, training, or simply relaxing around the stadium.

Beyond serving as a sports venue, the stadium also plays an important role in hosting major university ceremonies, athletic tournaments, and community events. It acts as a gathering place where students come together for sports days, fitness activities, and even large-scale celebrations. The wide surrounding area provides space for group workouts, marching band practices, and recreational exercise, contributing to an energetic and lively atmosphere. As a landmark that symbolizes KKU’s growth and spirit, the 50th Anniversary Stadium is both a functional facility and a meaningful part of the university’s identity.

Srinagarind
Hospital



Srinagarind Hospital, located within Khon Kaen University’s Faculty of Medicine, is one of the most advanced and well-known medical centers in Northeast Thailand. It serves as the main teaching hospital for medical students, nurses, and other health science programs, providing them with hands-on clinical experience in a real healthcare environment. The hospital is equipped with modern medical technology and a wide range of specialized departments, allowing it to deliver high-quality treatment and care to patients from Khon Kaen and nearby provinces.

The hospital functions as a regional referral center, meaning patients with complex or severe medical conditions from other hospitals are often transferred here for advanced diagnosis and treatment. It houses specialized units such as the cancer center, trauma center, cardiovascular clinics, and advanced surgery departments. Because of its comprehensive services, Srinagarind Hospital plays a crucial role in improving public health in the entire northeastern region.

In addition to medical care, the hospital is heavily involved in research and innovation. Doctors and researchers from the Faculty of Medicine frequently conduct studies that contribute to Thailand’s medical advancements, such as new treatment approaches, public health solutions, and disease prevention strategies. These research activities help ensure that the hospital remains a leader in modern medicine, offering patients the most updated and effective care possible.


Beyond its clinical and academic roles, Srinagarind Hospital is also designed to support patients and families during challenging times. The campus includes waiting areas, food stalls, convenience stores, and green spaces to make long hospital visits more manageable. Many volunteers and staff members work to create a warm, supportive atmosphere, helping patients feel more comfortable and cared for. Altogether, Srinagarind Hospital stands as a center of healing, learning, and community support within Khon Kaen University.
